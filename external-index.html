Overview
Six one-hour sessions

A teaching method you can actually use.

Six practical sessions on the techniques that make the biggest difference in any classroom — clear, transferable, and ready to use on Monday. Each live session has a self-guided companion page with worked detail, a scenario tool, and a coaching task.

How it works. Each session is delivered live in an hour, then supported by the companion page here — about an hour of self-guided detail you can return to whenever you need it. Work through them in order, or revisit any one as a reference. Nothing here is specific to one school; every technique transfers to your context.
