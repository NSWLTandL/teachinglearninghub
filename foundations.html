The Foundations.

Five short reading units on the things every routine rests on — how you carry yourself, how you speak, how you conduct yourself, and how you hold steady. Read each in a single sitting, and return to them across the year.

These units are reflective rather than observable — there is no checklist and no coaching partner needed. They sit beneath the six pillars of the programme, and are worth reading before, and alongside, the modules themselves.
Unit 1
Assertiveness
Carrying authority calmly — giving an instruction and holding the line without escalation.
Unit 2
Presence
What the room reads before a word is spoken: position, stillness, gaze, and pace.
Unit 3
Professional Conduct
The basics of conduct, professionalism, and co-working that hold the school together.
Unit 4
Voice
The instrument you use most — projection over volume, silence as a tool, and looking after it.
Unit 5
Composure
Staying measured when a moment invites otherwise: the pause between provocation and response.
Man Neeva, Mat Uchi
