04 · How to use this matrix

Three audiences. Three reads.

The matrix exists for distinct reasons depending on who is reading it.

FOR TEACHERS

"What does this checklist item mean?"

Find the item ID. Follow the link to the module. The module contains the script, the rationale, the deliberate practice, and the coach prompt for that item.

FOR COACHES

"Where do I send a colleague who failed BFL3?"

Look up BFL3 in the items list. The Module column shows the canonical home of that item. The colleague rehearses the relevant practice slot in that module before re-observation.

FOR LEADERS

"Where are we in the build?"

The Status column on every row shows what is built, what is in build, and what remains planned. The stats at the top give the headline numbers.
