The programme

Six pillars of classroom practice.

The Teaching Excellence Programme is organised into six pillars. Each module maps to the school's Teaching & Learning checklist and is built for direct instruction: step-by-step, visible, and clear. Pillars 2 and 6 are deferred for now and appear greyed.

The strand beneath the pillars

Foundations.

Five self-directed reading units on the dispositions the routines rest on. No checklist, no coaching partner — read in a single sitting, returned to across the year.

Unit 1AssertivenessCarrying authority without escalation. Unit 2PresenceWhat the room reads before a word is spoken. Unit 3Professional ConductThe basics of conduct, professionalism, and co-working.
