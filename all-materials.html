Printable materials

Download the booklets and decks.

Every pillar and the Foundations strand has a participant booklet and a facilitator deck. Use them for in-person CPD, or as a printed companion to the self-guided pages online. Click any item to download it.

Booklets are Word documents (.docx); facilitator decks are PowerPoint files (.pptx). Each booklet opens with a short reading on the thinking behind the pillar, and pairs with its deck for a facilitated session.
